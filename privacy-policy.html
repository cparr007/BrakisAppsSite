Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026

BrakisApps ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Data you provide: Information you voluntarily enter into our apps, such as recipes, notes, tasks, or settings preferences. This data is stored locally on your device and, if you enable iCloud sync, in your private iCloud account.
  • Camera and photo access: Some apps (such as RecipeCast) request access to your camera or photo library to scan and extract text using on-device OCR via Apple Vision. Photos and images are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
  • We do not collect location data, contacts, or device identifiers.

Data Storage and Security

Your app data is stored locally on your device. If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private iCloud account managed by Apple, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use Apple's built-in frameworks (such as CloudKit and SwiftData). These services are governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy. We do not integrate third-party advertising, analytics, or social media SDKs.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Subscriptions and Auto-Renewal

Some of our apps offer optional subscriptions that unlock premium features. Subscriptions are billed through the Apple App Store and automatically renew at the end of each billing period (weekly, monthly, or annually, depending on the plan you select) unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

  • Payment: Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase.
  • Renewal: Your subscription automatically renews unless you turn off auto-renewal in your Apple ID account settings at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Cancellation: You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time by going to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • Price changes: Subscription prices may change. If a price increase occurs, Apple will notify you and request consent before charging the new price.

We do not collect or store any payment information. All billing is handled entirely by Apple.

CloudKit Family Sharing

Some apps (such as RecipeCast) allow you to share content with family members using Apple's CloudKit sharing infrastructure. When you share content:

  • Shared data is transmitted and stored through your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit framework.
  • Only the people you explicitly invite can access shared content.
  • We do not operate any intermediary server — sharing happens directly between iCloud accounts managed by Apple.
  • We have no access to shared content or to the identities of participants in a shared zone.

Family sharing is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and iCloud terms.

Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account, you have full control over it. You can delete your data at any time by deleting content within the app or by uninstalling the app.

Your Rights Under GDPR (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access: You may request confirmation of whether we process your personal data and, if so, request a copy.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we restrict processing of your personal data.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to processing of your personal data.

Because our apps store data locally on your device and in your private iCloud account, you already have direct control over all of your data. To exercise any of these rights or if you have questions, contact us at chris@brakisapps.com.

Your Rights Under CCPA (California)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with the following rights:

  • Right to know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We never have and never will.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

Because we do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to our servers, there is minimal personal information for us to disclose or delete. Your app data is under your control on your device and iCloud account. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at chris@brakisapps.com.
