Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, a copy is also stored in your private iCloud account. We do not store your data on our servers.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual items within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all local data. To remove iCloud data, go to Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage on your device.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed through the Apple App Store. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID.

Which devices are supported?

Our apps are designed for iPhone and require iOS 17.0 or later.

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at chris@BrakisApps.com with a description of the issue, the app name, your iOS version, and your device model. Screenshots are helpful.
